Problem Statement

Currently, small businesses are unable to leverage the use of self-checkout systems to reduce their long checkout lines and labor expenses due to the high price tag on self-checkout equipment ($30,000 per kiosk)

Our Solution is to build a grocery store computer vision checkout system through a website.
The entire self-checkout is done on customers phones hence making it a relatively cheap and inexpensive for small businesses
